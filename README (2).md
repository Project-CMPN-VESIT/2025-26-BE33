# 🍃 NutriVision AI

AI-powered food & health intelligence. Upload a food photo or speak your meal — get instant nutrition analysis personalised to your health condition.

## Features
- 📸 Food image recognition (Swin-V2)
- 🎙️ Voice food detection (Whisper)
- 🏥 Personalised health check (Diabetes, BP, Cholesterol, etc.)
- 📊 Macro & risk nutrient charts
- 🎆 Fireworks animation for safe foods!

## Setup
Upload your `food_nutrients.csv` to the Space files before running.
